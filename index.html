Evan Hecht

Arts, Entertainment and Culture Journalist

Evan Hecht is a junior journalism major and arts leadership minor at the University of Maryland. Born and raised in Northern New Jersey, he grew up surrounded by the arts and entertainment industry and fell in love with everything about it.

Through his passion for writing and love of pop culture, he became a journalist.

Currently, Evan writes for The Diamondback's Diversions desk as well as producer Drop the Pop for WMUC news. You can also listen to his own radio show, Happy Songs for Sad B's, on WMUC digital every Wednesday at 5 p.m.
